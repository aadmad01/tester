About Us

How We Started:

Adopt A Dog is a shelter that is driven to find all dogs a new home. We believe that all dogs deserve a loving owner regardless of their past. We founded the project 53 years ago, in 1989, after seeing many stray dogs wandering the streets, our founders decided to band together to create Adopt A Dog. We are proud to be one of the largest organizations committed to finding homes for thousands of dogs.

Our Goals:

At Adopt A Dog, we want to foster a community of individuals to help take care of the many dogs in need.
